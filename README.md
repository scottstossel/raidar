# raidar
AI Research Radar for Evaluation, Safety, and Agent Systems

raidar is a research intelligence platform that monitors emerging work in key areas of modern AI. It ingests research signals from sources like arXiv and GitHub, processes them using distributed data pipelines, and surfaces trends through structured insights and grounded research briefs.

The goal of raidar is to help engineers and researchers quickly understand what ideas are gaining momentum in rapidly evolving AI domains.
